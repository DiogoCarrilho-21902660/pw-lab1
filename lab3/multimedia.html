Poema sobre Roma

-Megan Alms-

"WHEN IN ROME"

When Venus came to tempt my love,
Some fervor I withdrew.
But my lover said, "When in Rome
do as the Romans do".
Minerva cried in vexation,
Juno I no longer knew,
Mars sought shelter in my thoughts
when Jupiter's cyclones blew.
But try as I did (hardly at all)
Venus would not subdue.
For the Roman said, "When in love,
do as the lovers do."
